Microeconomic Analysis: Q1 FY24-25 (April–June 2024)
Agriculture & Rural Consumption:
Agricultural growth hit 3.5%, aided by a strong monsoon. MGNREGA demand fell 0.2% y-o-y. CPI inflation eased to 4.83%, boosting rural spending.
Services Sector:
Grew 7.2%, with services exports up 21.3% to $216 billion (Apr-Oct 2024). PMI at 58.7 reflected firm growth. GDP deflator ~2-3%, aiding profitability.
Manufacturing & MSMEs:
IIP growth at 5.7%, with manufacturing at 5.2%. WPI inflation ~1.5% eased MSME costs. PLI drove $15.2 billion in investments.
Employment:
Unemployment at 4.1%. EPFO added 11.2 lakh jobs monthly. LFPR rose to 37.5% (females).
Consumer Behavior:
Private consumption at 60.5% of GDP. Retail sales grew 6.5% y-o-y. Rural expenditure up 5.2%.
Automotive Industry:
Two-wheeler production up 18.5%, sales up 21.8% y-o-y.
Interest Rates: Repo rate stable at 6.5%. Trade deficit $70.8 billion.
Global Analysis
Global GDP: Grew 3.6% (2024), projected 3.4% (2025), EMDEs at 4.8%.
Inflation: Global CPI at 5.7% (2024), U.S. 3.2%, Eurozone 2.7%.
Trade: World trade up 1.2% (2024), oil prices down 8% y-o-y.
FDI: Global inflows $1.38 trillion (2024), India’s share 2.7%.
